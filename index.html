Street Light Management System for Dark Ecological Corridors in Bristol

Artificial Light at Night (ALAN) is a global phenomenon that poses a major threat to biodiversity. Bristol City Council challenged us to optimize Street Light Management to create dark ecological corridors. Our solution is able to increase bat activity by 10%, save about 185 tons of emitted CO2, as well as £25,000 in electricity.
